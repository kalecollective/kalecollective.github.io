About Kale Collective

We are Kale Collective, a London-based startup on a mission to make our cities more liveable by empowering every business to make their operation sustainable.

Making cities green takes more than electrifying vans on the street - particle emissions, road congestion, and road casualties remain big problems. Cargo bikes are the real future for urban last-mile deliveries, but the change won’t happen without redesigning the way urban deliveries happen.

Kale Collective is building the logistics optimisation platform where businesses can make their transition to cargo bike with confidence – backed with data.

Approach

  1. Maximise green return on investment. Cargo bike deliveries don’t need to be more expensive than legacy delivery services. We lower the barrier of green logistics for businesses through operational efficiency.

  2. Quantify and reduce uncertainty. Data from urban environment is noisy. We actively embed the uncertainty into our model, make it resilient to unexpected variance.

  3. Efficiency through diversity, not centralisation. We optimise to actively facilitate the ecosystem of small-medium businesses within the larger logistics landscape.
